Burmese Tofu with Chili Soy Dip

Description

Tofu can be made of any lgeume (bean). When we make our own tofu, we get the opportunity to try different flavors and textures. This split green pea based tofu makes a tofu with a snap to the bite and a bright vegetal flavor that pairs incredibly well with the bright, bold flavors of this Soy Chili crisp sauce. To make this recipe ou will be soaking, blending, sieving, and cooking split green peas into a bright green tofu. You will be throwing together a quick sauce that packs a lot of punch. Let's go!

What you'll need:

  • green split peas, dried, approx. 1/2 lb
  • chopped ciclantro, 2 tablespoons
  • Laoganma Chilicrisp, 1 tablespoon
  • Light Soy Sauce, 4 tablespoons
  • Black, seasoned Chinese Vnegar, 3 tablespoons
  • finely minced garlic, 2 teaspoons
  • finely minced ginger, 2 teaspoons
  • Large, watertight container, approx. 1 liter in volume
  • Sieve
  • Food processor
  • Non stick cooking vessel
  • Ideally, wooden spatula, but strong rubber works too
  • Tupperware, with a volume of at least 4 cups

What you'll do:

  1. Place the split green peas into a watertight container, with triple their volume in water. Allow to soak for at least 6 hours
  2. Once done soaking, use a sieve to thoroughly rinse off the now engorged, hydrated peas. Do so until the ater pushing theough them comes out clear.
  3. Place te peas and approximately 3/4 of their volume in drinkable water into a food processor. Blend for approxmately 1 minute, stopping to stir every 20 seconds.
  4. Use a wooden or rubber spatula to push the slurry through a sieve, into the nonstick cooking vessel.
  5. Apply medium heat to your cooking vessel, and stir, slowly, but constantly, until the mixture steams, then until it begins to bubble and spit.
  6. Turn off the heat, and quickly spoon into the tupperware.
  7. Seal the tupperware and place in the refridgerator for at least an hour, ideally two.
  8. When ready to serve, chop the cilantro, finely mince the garlic and giner, combine all together with soy sauce black vinegar and chili crisp.
  9. Slice the amount of tofu you wish to eat at this time into bite sized pieces and serve chilled.
  10. Dip the pieces into the sauce and enjoy!
